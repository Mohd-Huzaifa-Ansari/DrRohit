About Us

Who is Dr. Rohit Pandey​ ?

Dr Rohit Pandey has done his MBBS from GMC, Miraj in Maharashtra through AIPMT, following which he was selected through AIPG having rank AIR-179, and took Orthopedics in UCMS & GTB Hospital Delhi.

He did his Senior residency for 3 years in UCMS and is inclined towards teaching & research. He went to Derby in United Kingdom for his Shoulder fellowship. Then he got selected for MacNep Arthroplasty fellowship in Sydney, Australia and worked there for 1 year with top 4 Arthroplasty Surgeons.

His mentor and guide Prof Rami Sorial with whom he learnt different techniques of Arthroplasty specially doing PSI CR TKR. He also worked with Dr Simon Coffey, Prof James Sullivan & Prof Craig Waller. He learnt Kinematic alignment, Anterior approach to hip, Robotic knee and hip replacement techniques.
